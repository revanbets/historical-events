════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  EVERY TIME YOU OPEN VS CODE — 3 STEPS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  STEP 1 — Open your project folder
  ──────────────────────────────────
  File → Open Folder → Select "Claude Research App"

  ⚠️  This is the most important step.
      Without it, Claude can't find your sessions
      or remember anything about your project.


  STEP 2 — Open the Terminal
  ──────────────────────────
  Press  Cmd + `  (the key above Tab, top-left keyboard)
  A black panel will appear at the bottom of VS Code.


  STEP 3 — Check you're logged in with Pro
  ─────────────────────────────────────────
  Type this and press Enter:

      claude auth status

  ✅ You're good if you see:
      "email": "joshstryker24@gmail.com"
      "subscriptionType": "pro"

  ❌ Problem if you see:
      "subscriptionType": null

      → Follow the fix below before doing anything else.

About This App
--------------
HistoDB is a private historical research database tool. Users can upload, tag,
and explore connections between historical events, people, and organizations.
The app connects to a shared backend database (Supabase) where research data
is stored and synced across devices.

This is a companion app to the desktop web version at:
https://historical-events-databse.netlify.app/

Face ID / Touch ID Usage
-------------------------
Face ID (and Touch ID) is used exclusively for local biometric authentication
to provide quick, secure login on the device. No biometric data is transmitted
to any server. The biometric check is handled entirely through Apple's
LocalAuthentication framework (expo-local-authentication) and only returns a
pass/fail result to the app.

Camera Usage
------------
The camera is used to photograph and scan physical documents for research
uploads. Photos are sent to our backend for text extraction (OCR) and are
not used for any other purpose.

Content Moderation (UGC)
-------------------------
Users can upload research content. A "Report Content" button is available on
every event detail screen, which opens an email draft to our moderation team.
A "Block User" option is also available. Reported content is reviewed within
24 hours.

Account Deletion
-----------------
Users can request full account and data deletion from:
Profile tab > Settings > Legal > Delete Account.
Deletion requests are processed within 30 days per our Privacy Policy.
